History of Space Exploration

The Space Race

The competition between the United States and the Soviet Union drove rapid advancement in space technology during the 1960s.

Key Milestones

  • 1957: Sputnik 1 - First artificial satellite
  • 1961: Yuri Gagarin - First human in space
  • 1969: Apollo 11 - First Moon landing
  • 1971: Salyut 1 - First space station
  • 1981: Space Shuttle - Reusable spacecraft
  • 1998: ISS construction begins
  • 2012: Curiosity lands on Mars
