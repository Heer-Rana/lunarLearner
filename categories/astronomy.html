Introduction to Astronomy

Astronomy is the scientific study of celestial objects, phenomena, and the universe as a whole. It encompasses everything from observing the night sky to understanding the nature of distant galaxies.

Modern Instruments

  • Optical Telescopes

Fields of Study

Planetary Science

Study of planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and other bodies in our solar system.

Cosmology

Study of the universe's origin, evolution, and large-scale structure.

Astrophysics

Study of the physical properties of celestial objects.
