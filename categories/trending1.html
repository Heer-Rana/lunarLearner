Next Full Moon
The next full moon, known as the Worm Moon, will occur on Friday, March 14, 2025, at 2:55 A.M. Eastern Time (ET)

For observers in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India (Indian Standard Time, IST), this translates to 1:25 P.M. IST on the same day.

Moonrise Time: In Ahmedabad, the moon will rise on the evening of March 14, 2025, at approximately 6:30 P.M. IST. This is an ideal time for observation, as the moon will appear large and luminous near the horizon.

The Worm Moon is traditionally named to signify the time when earthworms emerge from the soil as the ground thaws, marking the approach of spring in the Northern Hemisphere.

By planning ahead and selecting a suitable viewing spot, you can experience the beauty and significance of the Worm Moon in March 2025.

Cultural Significance: The Worm Moon holds various cultural meanings across different societies. Exploring local traditions and folklore can provide a deeper appreciation of this lunar event.

Night Sky

Planetary Alignments and Visibility

Venus: The "Evening Star" will be prominent in the western sky after sunset, shining brightly and easily visible to the naked eye.

Jupiter and Saturn: Both gas giants will rise late in the evening and can be observed in the eastern sky. Telescopes will reveal Jupiter's Galilean moons and Saturn's rings.

Mars: Visible in the early morning hours, glowing red and steadily moving eastward.
