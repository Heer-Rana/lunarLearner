Exoplanets: New Worlds Beyond

Explore the fascinating discoveries of planets orbiting distant stars and what they teach us about our cosmic neighborhood.

Exoplanets, or planets outside our solar system, have become a focal point of astronomical research. With thousands of confirmed exoplanets, scientists are studying their atmospheres, compositions, and potential habitability.

These discoveries not only expand our understanding of planetary systems but also raise questions about the possibility of life beyond Earth. As technology advances, we are poised to learn even more about these distant worlds.
